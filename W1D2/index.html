Scrambled Eggs

  • Ingredients
  • Preparation

Description

This is an awesome recipe for scrambled eggs!

Recipe Link

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Ketchup

Prep Section

  1. Heat your pan with oil.
  2. Break open your eggs.
  3. Stir the eggs to pieces until scrambled
  4. Add salt and pepper ans serve them hot!!!
